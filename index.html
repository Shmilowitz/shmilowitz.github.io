Who Am I?

I'm a cybersecurity professional working at the intersection of offensive security, detection engineering, compliance, and technical sales. My goal is to understand threats deeply enough to build defences that actually work — and to communicate that value clearly to the people who need it.

I've spent the past two years in detection engineering and NDR/SIEM innovation at Muninn and Logpoint, designing attack simulations to validate and improve detection logic, and acting as a subject-matter expert during pre-sales and customer engagements.

I thrive where regulation, technology, and communication meet — whether that's on an audit, a sales call, or a threat simulation. Let's talk.
