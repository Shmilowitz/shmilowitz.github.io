01 / About

Who Am I?

I'm a cybersecurity professional with a focus on offensive security, compliance, and technical pre-sales. My goal is to understand threats deeply enough to build defences that actually work, and to communicate that clearly to whoever needs to hear it.

I've spent the past two years in detection engineering and NDR/SIEM innovation at Muninn and Logpoint, designing attack simulations to validate and improve detection logic, and acting as a subject-matter expert during pre-sales and customer engagements.

Currently I work as a Cyber Security Specialist at Fødevarestyrelsen, where I supervise NIS2 compliance across Denmark's veterinary and food sector, conducting technical audits, assessing governance frameworks, and helping regulated entities build resilience.

Whether that's a threat simulation, a compliance audit, or a pre-sales call, the underlying skill is the same: understanding how systems fail and making that legible to the people in the room.
